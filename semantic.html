Paneer Tikka

This delightful vegetarian dish is a popular Indian appetizer. It features marinated paneer (Indian cheese) and a burst of flavors.

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tikka masala spice mix
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, cut into chunks
  • Skewers for grilling

Instructions

  1. Prepare the marinade by mixing yogurt, tikka masala spice mix, ginger-garlic paste, and lemon juice in a bowl.
  2. Add the paneer cubes, bell pepper, and onion chunks to the marinade. Coat them well and let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Thread the marinated paneer, bell pepper, and onion onto skewers.
  4. Grill the skewers until the paneer and vegetables are slightly charred and cooked through.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney or a squeeze of lemon juice.
